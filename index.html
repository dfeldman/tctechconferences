Twin Cities Tech Events

Tech events in Minnesota/Twin Cities!

A few select meetups are included, but I don't intend to copy every meetup off meetup.com.

Want to add your group or see an error? Email dfeldman.mn@gmail.com, or send me a pull request directly.

General

  • Minnebar April -- massive tech community "unconference"
  • Minnedemo 3 times a year, next is July 14 -- 7-minute live startup product demos
  • MHTA April -- where the bigger local tech companies get together
  • eyeo June -- Minnesota's version of SxSW. Costs $700 and sells out in minutes.
  • Tech Cities February -- Conference on tech talent and retention to continue to bill Minnesota as the Tech Capital of the North!
  • Maker Faire May -- makers show off their projects
  • Ignite Minneapolis quarterly -- TED-like talks with a techy bent
  • TEDxMinneapolis August -- actual TED talks with a techy bent
